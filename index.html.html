Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve?

We provide facility management services primarily across Delhi NCR — New Delhi, Gurugram, Noida, Faridabad and surrounding regions. We also work with clients across other locations on a case-by-case basis. Contact us to discuss your specific location and requirements.

How quickly can you respond to emergencies?

Our emergency response team is available 24/7 with a guaranteed 1-hour response time for critical facility issues. For Battery & UPS emergencies, we maintain a 1-hour SLA. For surveillance incidents, our response time is under 20 minutes.

Do you offer customized service packages?

Yes, absolutely. We customize services to match your facility's specific needs and budget. Our integrated approach allows flexible combinations — you can choose individual verticals or our full-stack facility management solution. We'll assess your facility and propose a tailored plan.

Can you manage multiple facility locations?

Absolutely. We currently manage 100+ facilities across multiple locations with centralized coordination and consistent quality standards. Our dedicated facility managers ensure uniform service delivery regardless of the number of sites.

What about payment and contract terms?

We offer flexible payment options and contract terms tailored to your business — monthly, quarterly, or annual contracts are available. Contact us for a detailed quote that fits your budget and operational cycle.

How do I get started?

Simply fill out the contact form above or call us directly at +91 98100 31191. We'll schedule a free facility consultation to assess your needs, after which we deliver a customized proposal within 48 hours. Onboarding is seamless with zero disruption to your operations.
